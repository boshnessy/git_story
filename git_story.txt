In 2-quart saucepan, stir together brown sugar and 1/3 cup whipping cream. 
Cook over medium heat 2 minutes, stirring constantly, until bubbly. 
Stir in vanilla and apple cider. 
Cook 10 minutes, stirring often, until thoroughly heated.
Serve cider in mugs. 
Top with whipped cream, caramel topping and/or cinnamon.
